Permissions & Location

EagleNav requires Location, Camera, Microphone, and Speech Recognition access. You can manage these anytime in Settings → EagleNav on your iPhone.

Obstacle Scan Not Working?

Make sure Camera and Microphone permissions are enabled. The Scan button is available on the map screen — tap it to activate.

Navigation Issues

EagleNav routing works best on the Cal State LA campus. Ensure Location is set to "While Using the App" or "Always" for best results.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

EagleNav ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Location data: Used in real time to provide turn-by-turn navigation. We do not store or transmit your location to any server.
  • Camera: Used only for on-device obstacle detection. Video frames are processed locally and never stored or uploaded.
  • Microphone & Speech Recognition: Used only for voice search. Recognized text is used locally to search campus buildings.
  • Bookmarks & preferences: Stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. Never transmitted externally.

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by EagleNav is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

EagleNav uses OpenStreetMap tile data for map rendering. Map tiles are fetched from public OpenStreetMap servers. Please refer to the OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy for details.

Data Retention

No personal data is retained by EagleNav beyond your current session. Bookmarks and settings are stored locally and can be cleared by deleting the app.

Children's Privacy

EagleNav is intended for college-age users and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
